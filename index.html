Who We Are

LightLine is a collective dedicated to transforming stations into safer, warmer, and more connected places through the power of light. Our mission is to bring comfort and togetherness where fear often lingers — starting at Astrid Square.

Our Concept

The LightBox — an interactive installation designed to create a beacon of warmth and safety. When people gather near it, it glows brighter, symbolizing unity and visibility in shared spaces.

Astrid Square, Antwerp

Our first edition takes place in one of Antwerp’s most iconic — yet often overlooked — public spaces. The LightBox stands as a symbol of hope, inviting people to connect, feel safe, and light up their surroundings together.
